Why Spongebob is Great

  1. Timeless
    • Unlike some shows, the humor is not dependent on popculture that largely dates it
    • Appeals to wide audiences. I liked it as a kid, still do now

  2. Humor
    • It is genuinely hilarious
    • At least the first four seasons. Don't quote me for episodes beyond that
    • Seriously. Tell me you can watch the episode where they think they kill the health inspector and not laugh

  3. Animation
    • Most of the time it's fairly cute and colorful
    • Randomly transitions into the gnarliest images to ever grace your eyeballs

  4. Simplicity
    • Not one brain cell is necessary to follow the plot
    • Mindless in all the best ways
